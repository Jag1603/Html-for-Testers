Prime Ministers of India

Ordered List (Chronological Order)

Here is a chronological list of Prime Ministers of India:

  1. Jawaharlal Nehru (1947-1964)
  2. Lal Bahadur Shastri (1964-1966)
  3. Indira Gandhi (1966-1977, 1980-1984)
  4. Morarji Desai (1977-1979)
  5. Charan Singh (1979-1980)
  6. Rajiv Gandhi (1984-1989)
  7. Vishwanath Pratap Singh (1989-1990)
  8. P. V. Narasimha Rao (1991-1996)
  9. Atal Bihari Vajpayee (1996, 1998-2004)
  10. H. D. Deve Gowda (1996-1997)
  11. I. K. Gujral (1997-1998)
  12. Manmohan Singh (2004-2014)
  13. Narendra Modi (2014-present)
